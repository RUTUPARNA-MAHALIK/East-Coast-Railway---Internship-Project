About Our Project

This internship project focuses on developing a comprehensive digital solution for East Coast Railway to improve operational efficiency and passenger experience. The system integrates modern technologies to streamline processes and enhance service delivery.

Our solution includes real-time train tracking, automated scheduling, passenger information systems, and maintenance management tools. The project was developed in collaboration with railway engineers and staff to ensure practical implementation.

Key objectives of this project include reducing delays, improving safety measures, optimizing resource allocation, and providing better information services to passengers.

Project Features

Key Features

Our internship project includes several innovative features designed to modernize railway operations

Real-time Tracking

Monitor train positions and schedules in real-time with our GPS-based tracking system.

Smart Ticketing

Integrated digital ticketing system with QR code scanning and contactless payments.

Automated Scheduling

Intelligent scheduling system that optimizes train routes and reduces delays.

Passenger Information

Real-time updates on train status, platform changes, and service announcements.

Maintenance Management

Predictive maintenance tools to ensure train safety and reduce downtime.

Performance Analytics

Detailed reports and analytics on railway operations for better decision-making.
